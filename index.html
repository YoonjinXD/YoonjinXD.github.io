Short Bio

Hi, my name is Yoonjin Chung and now I’m working as an AI researcher at Gaduio Lab. Previously, I obtained an M.S. degree in the Graduate School of AI from KAIST in 2023, advised by Prof. Juhan Nam. My research interest is usually in a general audio generation, sound event detection and music information retrieval!
